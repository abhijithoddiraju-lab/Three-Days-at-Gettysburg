Quick Start Guide

  • Movement: Use W A S D to navigate.
  • Combat: Aim with Mouse and Left-Click to fire.
  • Reload: Press R. Powder weapons take time.

Armory Strategy

  • The battlefield demands total versatility.
  • CRITICAL: To survive later waves, make sure you upgrade BOTH your rifle and your pistol! Relying on just one weapon leaves you completely vulnerable during reloads.
